Interactive Hundreds Charts

By Shiela Lee

This is an interactive hundreds chart for primary school teachers. Be sure to enlarge your window to the maximum. Try clicking the numbers! When you want to highlight a particular pattern on the hundreds chart (e.g. even numbers or landmark numbers), simply click the electric plug to help children see the patterns on the hundreds chart.

Try out some of the buttons on the side to help you highlight patterns.
